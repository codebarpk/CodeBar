Hackerspace For Learning

CodeBar is part of supply and demand solution for education and training sector. We are not your typical institute who works 24/7 and only interested in pushing big batch of students to market with outdated knowledge and without thinking if there are more jobs for them or not. We are offering courses that are vital and current to meet industry demands our measure of success is not around how many enrolled but how many got jobs after taking our classes.

We are trying to avoid typical business model of education and training sector in every way possible to stay lean, meaningful to student and bridge the gap among academia and industry. Our target audience are students, professionals and industry.

Turning Student Into Professionals

We have founded CodeBar to help students acquire new skills and tools, improve their craft to meet industry demands and score job easily. Our measure of success for a course is not driven by number of students enrolled but how many of our students filled the openings in local industry. Please check our blog for more details and updates about course contents.
